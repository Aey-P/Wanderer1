Hua Lamphong🚂

Bangkok (Hua Lamphong) railway station , RTGS: Sathani Krung Thep (Hua Lamphong)) is a railway station in Pathum Wan, the former central passenger terminal in Bangkok and the former railway hub of Thailand. (Wikipedia)

Personally, Hua Lamphong gives off such a nostalgic vibe that I miss from my daily life. The food, handicrafts, culture, and traditional lifestyle that have existed for over a century, everything about it just feels warm and inviting. If you’re looking to spend a 'wander'ful day soaking in some art and sipping on a delicious cup of coffee, this might be your perfect place.
